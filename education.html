Education

As an organization we give proper importance to education. Because for us education is one of the most agent of development in society. Our target group is educationally deprived section of society who are away to taste the fruit of development as they are lack of education. Our core thinking is to enable an individual to stand on its own for earning suitable livelihood.

For us education is a medium to transform society, hence our Acharyas /Teachers serve with a social commitment

Our academic activities are operational in 30 provinces of the country

70555 girls and boys are receiving education in our plenary schools.

Banswara,Bewar of Rajsthan and Tripura are the locations where maximum schools of our organization are functioning.


To create quality citizens children are imparted training on both development of knowledge, intelligence and character.

Banswada project under its “Bal gokulam scheme’’ has adopted 135 children from tribal families. The project takes care of food, clothing, education, accommodation of the adopted children.

Hostels for needy students are running by organization in states like Rajsthan, Gujarat, Odisha, Maharastra, Tripura, Mizoram etc.

Computer training centres are dedicated for developing the capability of students for both educational and professional requirements.

To summarize the work in figure:

  • 1236 Schools
  • 46 Hostels
  • 225 Libraries
  • 22 Computer training centres
  • 355 Child development centres.
